Room 14

You decide that checking the rooms is worth the time. You test a few knobs and find them all locked. Alex begins to rifle through the patient files kept in plastic file organizers mounted next to each door. Is that okay? You suppose under such dire circumstances it can’t be that big of a deal. Besides, they’d wouldn’t leave the important files just out like this, would they? You cautiously begin to rifle through another patient’s papers when Alex suddenly yells an “Aha!” They turn to you abruptly and wave three charts in your face.

“Lookit! This one’s different! They marked the birthday, and with a different color pen at that. You think this means something? Like a code?” But a code for what? You need to explore more.
