You wait for the pounding and crying to settle down. Something weighs heavy on your chest, and anxiety flutters in your stomach, but eventually you click the lock back open on the door. It swings open abruptly and knocks you back onto the stairs. Alex looms over you, dimly backlit by the lights from the previous hall.

“How could you betray me? After all I’ve done for you.” No light reflects in their eyes.

“Alex, wait!” You beg, curling away defensively.

Your words mean nothing. Alex grips the handle of the once whole mallet and clouts you in the back of the head.
